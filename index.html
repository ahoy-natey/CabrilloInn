About Our Inn

Nestled along Santa Barbara’s stunning coastline, The Cabrillo Inn at the Beach is your gateway to the California beach life. Our classic motel offers clean, comfortable rooms and a retro vibe that’s as welcoming as the ocean breeze.

Whether you’re here for a sun-soaked getaway, a family adventure, or just a good night’s rest on your road trip, you’ll enjoy easy access to the beach, local attractions, and the timeless charm of Santa Barbara.

Our friendly staff is here to make your stay relaxed and memorable. Discover why generations of travelers have called The Cabrillo Inn their home by the sea.
